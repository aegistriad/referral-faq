What is a referral system?

A referral system is a way to help people get involved in a meaningful way to help promote services and products, in our case it's a pressure washing service.

When someone signs up to become a referrer, they will get a unique link that identified only to them. When someone uses that link to book a job, claim a offer, coupon, or get a quote, the referrer is credited that interaction.

An incentive is often used to for people to help promote and share the link, in this case the incitive is cash.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy, simply go to the offer page, or to the referrer sign up page, and sign up. Once signed up, a referral link will be generated automatically, you will also recieve an email that will contain the link.

One you have your link, you can start sharing it and generating earnings.

What's the best way to share my link?

The best way to share your link is with the following platforms and services, facebook post, twitter, instagram, tex messaging, facebook messenger, email, and many more places.

Typically starting with people you know, like friends, family, co-workers, and people in your network is the most effective way to get started, once some momentum gets going, the link will start to circulated by other people and can be become almost automatic.

How does the payout work?

There are 3 metrics we track from a referrer, clicks, referrals, and bookings. A click is when someone clicks on a link that you share, a referral is when someone claims a offer or coupon, and a booking is when someone books a job and job gets started.

The payouts go as follows clicks pay 10 cent per click, referrals, pay $1.00 each, and bookings pay $50 for the first 3 and $10 per booking after that. To help eliminate gaming the system, clicks and referrals coming from the same ip or device will not be counted, and the account may be terminated.

How do I get paid?

Payouts can be check by mail, zelle, or paypal. Payouts happen for every $50 you earn on the 1st and 15th of every month. Upon reaching the $50 threshold, you will have the option to either take payment or hold funds in your account, by holding in your account you can request full payment no matter the amount as long as it's over $50

How do I track my payouts?

Upon signing up an acccount page will be generated, this page will provide live tracking, earnings, payout history, and provide some tools and resources to help you be successful.

Alternativley you can always go back to the page or offer you signed up with, re-enter your info, and real time stats will appear at bottom of page.
